SIMAPATDA
SIMAPATDA is an integrated application module designed to oversee tax management for Dinas Pendapatan Daerah. This comprehensive module includes features such as taxpayer registration, advertisement registration, taxpayer card issuance, payment letters, and reporting functionalities.
E-Billing
An integrated system, developed in collaboration with SIMAPATDA and major banking providers, provides Batam citizens with a seamless solution for generating billing codes for various tax payments. This encompasses a wide array of services such as billboards, restaurants, hotels, entertainment, parking, PPJU, and MBLB. This groundbreaking platform streamlines the tax payment process, ensuring users experience efficiency and accuracy, ultimately facilitating timely payments. This, in turn, contributes significantly to the smooth functioning of the local economy.
E-Filling
An administrative system is in place to manage file archives based on file types, allowing for easy filtration by division. The E-Filling system also oversees the management of incoming and outgoing letters, a process handled by the Secretariat Division from the Division Chief to the staff level.
SIEPENDA
A dashboard application is seamlessly integrated with SIMPATDA, enabling the generation of comprehensive information on various aspects, including PAD realization, local tax realization, regional distribution realization, targets, and realization, as well as regional tax and retribution targets and realizations.
BENDONLINE
An integrated system collaboratively operating with SIMAPATDA is in place to manage the collection of Retribusi Daerah payments and generate financial reports utilized by various local government departments.
ESIPUGA
A planning system for Dinas Pemberdayaan Perempuan Batam which want to create a database for their survey data regrading implementation of woman empowerment and child protection law.
SISBAPER
An application system is in place to efficiently manage goods based on SKPD and its sub-units. This system facilitates various transactions, including receiving, distributing, requesting, and taking goods. Additionally, it generates comprehensive reports for each transaction to ensure transparent and organized record-keeping.
E-PENDAFTARAN

An integrated system, in collaboration with SIMAPATDA and leading bank providers, offers Batam citizens a seamless solution to generate billing codes for their tax payments, covering a range of services including billboards, restaurants, hotels, entertainment, parking, PPJU, and MBLB. This innovative platform simplifies the tax payment process for users and ensures accurate and timely payments, contributing to the smooth functioning of the local economy.
